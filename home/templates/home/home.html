Welcome to Phishing Net!

The innovative, crowd-sourced phishing email detection tool. PhishingNet is a website where you post suspicious emails and get feedback! You'll upload a screen shot of a potential phishing email. Then PhishingNet will assign the post to subject matter experts depending on what type of email you uploaded. Then the experts will vote and comment on whether they think your email is phishing or not.

How to get started

Click on upload and upload a screenshot of the email and check back in a few hours! You can also click on assigned to vote and comment on posts made by your peers.
